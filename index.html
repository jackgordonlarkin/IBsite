61 years of Stupidity

Inward Bound is a unique adventure footrace, challenging the physical and emotional endurance of participants and providing safe and exciting opportunity to explore the Australian wilderness. Somehow it continues to run into it's 58th edition (Yes we know, it's been cancelled a few times lately as well as in 2008).
